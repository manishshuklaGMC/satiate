Management Team

As Omax Security Services Pvt. Ltd, we understand the security is a manpower business and appreciate the state and central role that personnel play in the delivery of better quality service. Our screening and hiring practices are designed to yield a human resource pool, which is superior to our competitors. The team ensures to provide Our clients and our employees with a single point of contract for responding to manage all their security/manpower needs in ordinary and emergency time, across India.

Omax Security Services Pvt. Ltd. Offers on-the-job training programs for all security personnel. Our commitment to training includes several customised, industry-specific programs. By training onsite, we can ensure the greatest level of quality security to our clients. We have our training centre at Lucknow and have ex-army and ex-paramilitary JCOs/ NCOs for imparting training.
