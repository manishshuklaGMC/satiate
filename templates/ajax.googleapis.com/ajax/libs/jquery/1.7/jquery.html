Welcome to Omax Security Services

We, the OSS do not believe in or subscribe to the "warm body syndrome," which is contrary to and a very common practice in the contract security industry. That is, we do not offer or provide "warm bodies" to work in any security related function for any clients. Each of our security personnel are fully qualified, experienced and trained and mostly drawn from Para military. We do not maintain minimum or sub-standard wages for our employees. In order to provide our clients with superior, reliable, dependable and professional high quality services, we must hire and retain experienced and highly qualified security personnel and pay them well.

Our Quality Policy

  • Omax Security Services Private Limited is an ISO 9001-2008 certified company lead by highly qualified professionals extracted from armed forces and private security agencies. This has been launched with a view to generate and provide well trained young and energetic personnel to afford desired safeguarding to private houses, firms, factories, vital installations and house keepings. We further introduce ourselves as the most professional security agency able to provide high quality services to the best satisfaction and requirement for our clients

  • As Omax Security Services Pvt. Ltd, we understand the security is a manpower business and appreciate the state and central role that personnel play in the delivery of better quality service. Our screening and hiring practices are designed to yield a human resource pool, which is superior to our competitors. The team ensures to provide Our clients and our employees with a single point of contract for responding to manage all their security/manpower needs in ordinary and emergency time, across India.
    Omax Security Services Pvt. Ltd. Offers on-the-job training programs for all security personnel. Our commitment to training includes several customised, industry-specific programs. By training onsite, we can ensure the greatest level of quality security to our clients. We have our training centre at Lucknow and have ex-army and ex-paramilitary JCOs/ NCOs for imparting training.
  • We as Omax Security Services Pvt. Ltd. endeavour to recognise code of practice within the security industry. We ensure that our "service quality standard" is exactly what the client has defined and agreed in formal contract.
    The Directors of Management of Omax Security Services Pvt. Ltd are fully committed to fulfil the requirements of better quality and remain fully aware of their job and responsibilities for the implementation of the quality assurance. Each staff member of the team is responsible for achieving the required level of quality in their security activity.
    Our goals are to consistently provide an outstanding service to our clients, ensuring that each client's requirements are always met and adhered to. This in turn, creates a positive business relationship between the client and the organisation.
